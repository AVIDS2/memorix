conclusion only after its evidence gate passes on the frozen confirmatory set.

## Forbidden shortcuts

- Deterministic retrieval-fixture success cannot establish coding-task success.
- Results on the Memorix repository cannot establish general coding-agent value.
- An LLM preference score cannot replace hidden tests for patch correctness.
- A single model, language, or repository cannot support a cross-agent claim.
- Post-hoc filtering cannot be presented as preregistered analysis.
- Baseline failures caused by broken installation or unequal budgets must not be counted as memory-system failures.
